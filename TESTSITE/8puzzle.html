How Does It Know The Way Out?

First of all, it is imperative to figure out whether the given puzzle is solvable or not. Luckily, for you I will be providing only those random board configurations which are solvable. Now, you might be wondering how did I do that. Well, 8Ly generates a random board and checks the number of inversions. If the number of inversions are EVEN, then it is solvable otherwise, not.

Coming to the AI part. Unlike the case of TicTacToe, 8Puzzle problem demands a more dedicated search-based solution approach. To implement it, 8Ly makes use of a clever heuristic search; Best First Search, to be precise.

Best First Search is a reasoning based state space tree traversal technique which relies on a strong logic, commonly called a heuristic. Heuristic is something which guides the AI through the most promising paths to reach its goal state. The heuristic used here to solve 8Puzzle is the Manhattan Distance.
