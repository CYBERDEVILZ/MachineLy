Why Can't I Beat It?

Many believe that Tic-Tac-Toe is a game of chance. But in reality, this is not the case. Any game, whose entire outcomes can be figured out, can easily be conquered. And Tic-Tac-Toe is one such game.

There are 255,168 unique games of Tic-Tac-Toe. Computers being fast, dont even take considerable amount of time to examine all those individual states. So, with that being said, lets see why you weren't able to beat the AI.

TicTacLy examines all the existing states of Tic-Tac-Toe and makes the move according to the human player's. TicTacLy has only been fed with the rules of the game as constraints. The move that it makes heavily depends on an algorithm called Minimax.

Minimax algorithm suggests the AI to play the most optimal move considering that the human player has already made an optimal move. So, whenever you try to win by matching the three Os together, you simply can't since the AI has already foreseen it and plays in such a way that you can't win. Interestingly, I haven't taught the AI to block or create traps. It figures it out on its own.
